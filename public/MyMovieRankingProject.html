Siraj's List of Best Movies

My personal favorites

A Beautiful Mind

This movie is special because it handles pieces fascets of human experience both the beautiful and tradgic. And how a relationship can flourish like a pheonix rising from its ashes and become stronger under the pressure of circumstance. A story of a man that has was handed a gift and a curse at the same time,and his ability with will and support to adapt and bring out the best in himself.

I Want to Eat Your Pancreas

One of the first Animes I've watched and a beautiful story of a young lady with cancer that has a unique relationship with an introverted young man. One thing that makes this movie special is the subtlety and innocence. It brings a beautiful dynamic between two contrasting personalities and shows just how much impact one person can make on your life.
